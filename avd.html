Analog vs Digital

Digital:

A digital synth is a complex computer program that controls an oscillator numerically. Despite at its core being 1's and 0's, digital synths allow the most control over oscillators. Digital synths are capable of making extremely complex waveforms. The power of modern personal computers and embedded computers have made digital synths extremely accessible.

Watch the video below to learn about one the most prolific digital synths of all time!

Analog:

An analog synth uses voltage controlled oscillators and modulators, making it a truly electronic instrument. Analog synthesizers are often described as having a warm and rich tone. Due to the amount of circuits needed to make an analog synth, they are generally more expensive. However, they tend to hold their longterm value much better compared to digital synths.
